B. Tech in Information System

WELCOME TO Information System DEPARTMENT

The specific objectives of the B.Tech. (Hons) Information System programme are to:

prepare a graduate for a career in the network management and general business systems area, not only from the technical aspects, but also the operational and management requirements;
develop the skills required to use a range of software and network tools and techniques in the development of effective systems across a range of domains;
equip the students with the capacity for professional practice in computing and communications;
develop in students through an education in Information System, a range of transferrable applicable IT skills to all aspects of human endeavours;
